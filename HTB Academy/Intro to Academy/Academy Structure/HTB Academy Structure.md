### Path
- Learning block that is made up of multiple modules

### Module
- Learning block that covers a specific self-contained topic

### Sections
- Smaller learning blocks that make up a module

Some paths are linked to certain certifications, which are obtained after completing the path and passing an exam.

![[structure2.png]]

It may look intimidating with all of the information, having it break up into these steps making the learning much more digestible and easier to understand with.

![[small_steps.jpg]]

Not only you can take one step at a time, but also ensures constant testing and challenging of your knowledge/skills
